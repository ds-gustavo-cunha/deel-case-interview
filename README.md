# **BUSINESS PROBLEM**


## **What is the business context of the project?**

**Deel clients may add funds to their Deel account using their credit and debit cards. Deel has partnered with Globepay to process all of these account funding credit and debit card transactions.** Globepay is an industry-leading global payment processor and is able to process payments in many currencies from cards domiciled in many countries.

**Deel has connectivity into Globepay using their API.** Deel clients provide their credit and debit details within the Deel web application, Deel systems pass those credentials along with any relevant transaction details to Globepay for processing.

## **What is the business problem the company is facing?**

<u>**Deel is experiencing a decline in the acceptance rate of credit and debit card payments processed by Globepay in the recent period.**</u>

<u>**The “acceptance rate” is defined as the number of accepted transactions divided by the total attempted transactions.**</u>

## **What is the business solution that this project has to deliver?**


**Task #1**

Please present a report (presentation/keynote/pdf) that:

1. **Outlines the volume (in USD) of the declined payments**
2. **Analyses the problem root causes**
3. **Suggests solutions / recommendations**
4. **Lists further next steps (hypothesis / analysis) you would do as a next steps having more time and business knowledge**

**Task #2**

Working on the same dataset, please provide pandas code to answer the following questions:
1. **What is the acceptance rate over time?**
2. **List the countries where the amount of declined transactions went over $25M**
3. **Which transactions are missing chargeback data?**
